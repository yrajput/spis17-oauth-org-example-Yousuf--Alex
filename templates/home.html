What is YAzZy?

Bring your wardrobe to life! Organize what you want, how you want it, whenever you want it. All with a click of a button.

With the help of Yazzy:

  • upload your photos onto a database for easy access
  • access your online closet from wherever you want
  • add personlized categories, creaing your perfect outfits for any event, whether it be your best friend's birthday party or a family vacation, or just based on the type of clothing
  • have the ability to store items of clothing in multiple categories with easy access
