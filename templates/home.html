Welcome to Yazzy!


Bring your wardrobe to life! Organize what you want, how you want it, whenever you want it. All with a click of a button.




With the help of Yazzy:

-Upload your photos onto a database for easy access

-Access your online closet from wherever you want

-Add personlized categories, creating your perfect
outfits for any event, whether it be your best friend's
birthday party or a family vacation, or just based on
the type of clothing




-Have the ability to store items of clothing in
multiple categories with easy access





To get started, click on the Login button in the top-right corner!






Yazzy is a Web Application project developed by the collaborative efforts of students at UC San Diego in the SPIS program
